What is bullying?

Bullying is an unwanted aggresive behavior that involves a real or perceived power imbalance and is repeated over time, causing harm to the victim.

Types of bullying

  • Disability Bullying
  • Sexual Bullying
  • Verbal Bullying
  • Cyber Bullying

    • Today we are going to touch on two topics, Disability and Sexual bullying

    What is Sexual Bullying?

    It's the time of bullying that involves aggression centered around a person's body, sexuality, or sex that is typically observed among adolescents

      How to recognize sexual bulling
      • Unwanted sexual comments or jokes
      • Spreading sexual rumors
      • Inappropriate touching
      • Sending or showing sexual content

          • How to stop sexual bullying
        • Build a whole school culture of respect
        • Teach consent & respectful relationships
        • Train staff & empower bystanders
        • Monitor & supervise school hotspots

          • What is disability bullying?

          Behavior or language that makes a person feel unwelcome, unsafe, or marginalized due to their real or assumed disability or additional need.

          How to stop disability bullying
          • Strong policies & clear reporting systems
          • Education, awareness & inclusive culture
          • Empower peer support & Bystanders
          • Engage parents, caregivers & the wider community
